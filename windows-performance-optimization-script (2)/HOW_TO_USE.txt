═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 2: OPEN IT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OPTION A (Easiest):
  • Double-click "index.html"
  • It opens in your browser
  • Done!

OPTION B (If double-click doesn't work):
  • Right-click "index.html"
  • Choose "Open with"
  • Select your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
  • Done!

OPTION C (Drag and drop):
  • Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
  • Drag "index.html" into the browser window
  • Done!

  STEP 3: USE IT!
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

When it opens, you'll see the Opnonti Pro interface.

Quick Setup for Free Fire:
  1. Click "Gaming" at the top
  2. Toggle "AI Drag Smoothing" ON
  3. Toggle "Free Fire Mode" ON
  4. Click the big "Activate" button
  5. Start playing!
